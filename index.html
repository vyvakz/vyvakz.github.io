Performance(20.03.2020 - 30.10.2021)

*Currently, the algorithm’s base currency is USDT. In this case, the algorithm’s goal is to increase USDT size and outperform BTC. We are working on implementing Coin-M Futures to our trading platform that will allow us to make Bitcoin a base currency for an algorithm in the future.
